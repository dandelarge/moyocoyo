With love from Mexico

The name Moyocoyo comes from the word Moyocoyotzin, which comes from the Aztec language and literally means ‘she who creates herself’.

We only use natural materials and make use of natural pigments. To keep our impact on the earth as low as possible.

100% Natural

All materials used are 100% natural. The fabric is a mix of cotton and bamboo and grows local. The dyes used in the garments are made of insects and plants.

Fair

Each product is made under fair circumstances. The women who created your garment works in a safe environment or from home and is being paid a fair salary.
